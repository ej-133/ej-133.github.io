Summary of Stamped

This book by Ibram X. Kendi and Jason Reynolds covers the story of African American presence within the United States, told as a history book which makes it seem like a Young Adult novel. This story is a simple novel that makes it easier for younger children to read, the explanation of American history from the African-American culture without intimidating a younger audience. This book goes through terms and different aspects that people might become normative to, but have racist connotations behind the terms or examples. For exmaple the movie Tarzan and the racail ideals that it holds, because it is consiting of a white man beating on the Black apes which make it seem like revisioning slavery. For this reason, some people can call it a history book, however, the authors Ibram X Kendi and Jason Reynolds would rather not until someone reads it.
