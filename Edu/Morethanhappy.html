More Than Happy Than Not

Summary More Than Happy than Not

More than Happy than Not, is a story that is based on Adam, a teenager living in the Bronx of New York. This setting makes it difficult for Adam when he is faced with coming out. The main plot of the story is based upon his coming out to his father and the reaction that was encountered after coming out to his father. As well as his friends did not create a safe environment where Adam felt comfortable with coming out and his grapple with living.

The different Characters

  • Aaron- is the main character
  • Elsie- is the mother of Aaron
  • Eric- is the older brother of Aaron
  • Mark- is the father of Aaron
  • Thomas- Aarons's best friend
  • Genevieve- the girlfriend of Aaron
