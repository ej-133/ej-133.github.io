Summary of Fire Keeper's Daughter

This Young Adult novel goes through the main character Daunis Fontaine a Native American teenage woman who recently turned 18 years old living on the Reservation with her mother. Daunis is a mixed-ethnicity daughter her dad being French and her mother benign Native American, because of this mixture people do not consider her fully Native American at times. She does not let this stop her from hanging out at the Reservation and playing on the hockey team with the other guys from the Reservation. However, the story hit a quick turnaround when her best friend dies due to a gunshot, hanging around a guy who deals drugs. This opens up a whole new world for Daunis because due to this she is met with an undercover cop who is investigating the Native American reservation for their drug usage. Which allows Daunis to be a part of the undercover informant inside the Reservation. This book allows for a bit of a mystery and drama series. Giving also the perspective of it what means to be a part of the Native American culture which might not be common for everyone.
