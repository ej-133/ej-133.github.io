Summary of Poet-X

The Adult Novel Poet-X is written in a poetic form, having every chapter being in the form of a poem about how the main character Xiomara Batista lives in the heart of Harlem, New York one of the most cultural and important places in New York. Xiomara a Latina woman at the age of fifteen starts to explore her identity and sexuality which diverges from her Mother’s teaching of Christianity and how to gain acceptance in heaven relying on women’s purity. However, when Xiomara gains the attention of love-interest such as Xavier a guy near the same age going to the same school. Her ideas of staying pure as her mother wants become questioned, and the only way of analyzing these questions was through the poems that she would write. This Adult Novel is more based upon the author’s experience so it has a lot of personal connections that the author has talked about. You get to understand
