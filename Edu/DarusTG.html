Summary of Darus the Great by

Darius the Great is not Okay is a young adult novel that goes through the adventures of the main character Darus an Arabian teenager living in Portland Oregon trying to find himself. Darius feels very displaced at the beginning of the book, having a struggling relationship with his father, and feeling distant from his sister Laleh. When Darius feels like nothing is going correctly that is when he and his family get invited to Yazd Iran by his grandparents. In this trip not only does Darius find out more about himself with a love between one of the friends he made in Iran, but also gets closer to his Dad. Sharing a moment where they both discuss how they have had suicidal scares and allowing their relationship to grow once they get back into Oregon. This book was enjoyable and has an open perspective of what it means to have someone from a different Country living in America and wanting to acclimate, but having struggles.
